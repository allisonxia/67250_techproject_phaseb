Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.

MonoMuse is a space where art and technology converge to inspire new ways of seeing and creating. Blending interactive media, digital installations, and contemporary artistic expression, the museum invites visitors to explore how innovation shapes creativity, and how creativity drives technological progress.

Through a curated mix of exhibitions, MonoMuse showcases works that span virtual reality, generative art, interactive design, and data-driven storytelling. Visitors are encouraged not just to observe, but to engage by experimenting with installations that respond to movement, sound, and touch. By highlighting collaborations between artists, engineers, and designers, the museum reveals the processes behind these works and the ideas that fuel them. Whether encountering a dynamic digital sculpture or exploring the aesthetics of artificial intelligence, guests leave with a deeper understanding of how technology expands the possibilities of artistic expression.
