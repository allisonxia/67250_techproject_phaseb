Mission Statement

MonoMuse believes that the relationship between science, art, and technology is always evolving. Our mission is to highlight the diverse voices and perspectives that make up our constantly evolving society.

Who this Museum Serves

Innovators. Creators. Artists. Citizens. Adults. Families. Children. And anyone else interested in the intersection of arts and technology!
